# What Is F7?

F7 is a **workforce intelligence platform** that helps organizations understand how their teams adopt and use AI tools. It provides objective, behavioral data — not surveys or self-assessments — to inform workforce strategy.

## The Problem F7 Solves

Organizations are investing heavily in AI tools but have no visibility into whether those investments are working. F7 answers three questions:

1. **Who are your most effective AI users?** Identify the people creating outsized value with AI tools.
2. **Who needs support?** Pinpoint individuals or teams that would benefit from targeted enablement.
3. **Which workflows are changing?** Understand how AI is reshaping work patterns at the organizational level.

## How F7 Is Different

| Traditional Approach | F7 Approach |
|---------------------|-------------|
| Annual surveys | Continuous behavioral observation |
| Self-reported tool usage | Actual usage patterns captured passively |
| Manager opinion | Objective, scored metrics |
| IT license logs ("activated") | Real workflow integration depth |

## Three Components

| Component | What It Does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Device Agent** | A lightweight agent on each machine that observes work patterns — app usage, AI interactions, workflow structure. Runs a local AI model to classify behavior on-device. No content ever leaves the machine. |
| **Controller** | A central server that receives structured metadata from agents, computes scores, and powers dashboards. Enforces tenant isolation and access control. |
| **Dashboard** | A web-based interface for executives and managers to view team analytics, AI adoption trends, and workforce insights. A personal dashboard for individual employees to access their own data is planned. |

## Who Uses F7

- **Executives (CTO, CHRO, CFO):** Strategic workforce planning, AI ROI measurement, headcount optimization
- **HR Leaders:** Skills gap analysis, training prioritization, change management
- **IT & Security:** Agent deployment, compliance verification, tool utilization reporting
- **Employees:** Personal productivity insights through a personal dashboard (planned)
